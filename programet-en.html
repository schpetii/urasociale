Programs

Clear work areas for an organization with many activities.

The program structure helps donors, schools, institutions and citizens understand how they can connect with URA Sociale.

Main programs

Four strong entry points for a large activity portfolio.

Quality education

Classroom observation, teacher meetings and recommendations for more inclusive teaching.

Life free from violence

Lectures, campaigns and advocacy on sexual harassment and gender-based violence prevention.

Social inclusion

Community activities for children and persons with disabilities, focused on dignity and participation.

Local advocacy

Cooperation with municipal directorates, schools and civil society networks for sustainable solutions.

01

Quality education

This program holds work with students, teachers and school leadership, including the verified work in "Lutfi Musiqi" and "Bahri Haxha" vocational schools.

  • Classroom inclusion observation
  • Recommendations for materials and methods
  • Training for sensitive topics
02

Life free from violence

This includes lectures on sexual harassment prevention, reporting pathways and discussions on gender-based violence.

  • Lectures for students aged 15-18
  • Information on reporting cases
  • Campaigns for equality and respect
03

Social inclusion

This program makes the 3 December inclusion work and community activities more visible.

  • Awareness and community activities
  • Cooperation with municipal directorates
  • Public message for equal opportunities
04

Local advocacy

Meetings with schools, psychologists, municipal directorates and partner organizations can be presented as a clear advocacy program.

  • Meetings with local institutions
  • Presentation of findings and recommendations
  • Partnerships for new projects
