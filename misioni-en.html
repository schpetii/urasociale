Mission

A social bridge for education, dignity and equal participation.

URA Sociale works where communities, schools and institutions need more communication, trust and practical action.

Who URA Sociale is

A development and communication organization based in Vushtrri.

Public sources list the organization as the Organization for Development and Communication "URA SOCIALE" (Social Bridge). The website presents it as a modern community-centered organization, close to young people, schools and local institutions.

Its public mission can be presented clearly: strengthening quality education, gender equality, violence prevention and social inclusion for groups that need stronger support.

Mission pillars

What visitors should understand in the first seconds.

Education is protection

When students understand rights, boundaries and reporting, schools become safer and fairer.

Equality is practiced

Gender stereotypes change through open discussion, sensitive teaching and responsive institutions.

Inclusion must be visible

Children and persons with disabilities should be present in public life, local activities and decisions.

Communication creates responsibility

Meetings with municipal directorates, schools and civil society turn issues into action plans.

Mission statement

To build bridges between young people, families, schools and institutions so every person can access education, safety, dignity and equal opportunity.
