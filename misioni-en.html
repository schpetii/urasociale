About Us

Ura Sociale

Ura Sociale is a non-governmental organization based in Vushtrri, Kosovo, dedicated to promoting youth empowerment, social inclusion, equality and community development.

Through education, advocacy, awareness campaigns and civic engagement initiatives, we support active participation and contribute to positive social impact.

Mission

Equal opportunities, stronger voices and more connected communities.

Ura Sociale works to create safe and inclusive spaces for young people, women and marginalized groups.

Through education, advocacy, awareness campaigns and cooperation with institutions and local communities, we promote quality education, gender equality, mental wellbeing and active citizenship.

Vision

A society where every young person feels seen, heard and supported.

We imagine an inclusive and progressive society where young people have equal access to opportunities, participation and personal development, regardless of gender or background.

Our goal is to build communities based on respect, equality and active citizenship, where young people are empowered to lead positive and sustainable change.
