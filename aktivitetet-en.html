18 December 2023

16 Days of Activism against Gender-Based Violence

URA Sociale held awareness lectures with young people from the Municipality of Vushtrri.

The lectures aimed to inform and inspire young people about gender equality, respect and prevention of every form of domestic and gender-based violence.

30 November 2023

Promoting quality education and transforming traditional gender norms

The activity included classroom observations in the "Bahri Haxha" and "Lutfi Musiqi" secondary schools in Vushtrri.

The findings were shared with teachers, directors and representatives of the Directorate of Education to discuss and advocate for improved teaching methods.

26 November 2022

International Day of Persons with Disabilities

URA Sociale, together with the Municipality of Vushtrri, organized activities focused on integrating students with special needs into society.

Through games, painting, dancing and entertainment, the activity aimed to create a special day for the children.

3 December 2021

We are all equal

On the International Day of Persons with Disabilities, URA Sociale implemented the project "We are all equal".

The project promoted acceptance of diversity and a more equal society through cultural and creative activities.

20 October 2022

Together we build hope

URA Sociale implemented three lectures within the "Together we build hope" subproject.

The subproject provided psychological and social services for the community, mainly secondary school students in Vushtrri.

14 January 2022

Sexual education and case reporting

URA Sociale implemented an activity with students of the "Eqrem Cabej" Gymnasium as part of the project on preventing sexual harassment.

The aim was to educate and inform young people about sexual harassment and how to report cases to the relevant institutions.

18 December 2021

Raising awareness for the prevention of sexual harassment

URA Sociale implemented the second lecture of Activity 3 within the project "Raising awareness for the prevention of sexual harassment".

Participants were students of SHMP "Bahri Haxha" in Vushtrri, with a focus on education, prevention and reporting.

6 October 2021

Volunteer cleaning of school yards

An activity with young people from Vushtrri for the voluntary cleaning of school yards.
