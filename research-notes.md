# URA Sociale Research Notes

Facebook page requested: <https://facebook.com/urasociale>

Direct unauthenticated fetching of Facebook was blocked, so the website content uses other public indexed sources. The Facebook URL is still linked from the site as the active social channel.

## Verified identity

- Public KWN records list the organization as: **Organization for Development and Communication "URA SOCIALE" (Social Bridge)**.
- Location listed publicly: **Deshmoret e Kombit str., no. 13, Vushtrri**.
- Representative listed publicly: **Mergime Jashari**.
- Source: Kosovo Women's Network annual report search result/PDF listing.

## Verified activity themes

- Gender equality and prevention of sexual harassment.
- Quality education and transformation of traditional gender norms.
- Youth awareness in secondary schools in Vushtrri.
- Cooperation with the Municipal Directorate of Education, school principals, psychologists and teachers.
- Inclusion activities for children/persons with disabilities around 3 December, the International Day of Persons with Disabilities.
- Local institutional cooperation with Vushtrri municipal directorates.

## Documented activities and numbers

- **2021-2022:** Five lectures with high school students in Vushtrri, reaching **125 students aged 15-18**, focused on sexual harassment prevention and reporting.
- **2023:** Concluding event for "Promotion of Quality Education and the Transformation of Traditional Gender Norms"; research/meetings involved approximately **300 students** from "Lutfi Musiqi" and "Bahri Haxha" vocational secondary schools.
- **2021 and 2022:** Public local-news coverage mentions URA Sociale cooperation in activities marking the International Day of Persons with Disabilities in Vushtrri.
